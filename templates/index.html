ScoutPilot Guide

Getting Started

Click + Add Search to create a search profile with your target job title. ScoutPilot automatically scrapes ALL search terms across LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, and 7+ sources every 5 minutes. Use Scrape Now to pull fresh results immediately.

What the Scores Mean

Relevance (Rel) shows how closely a job matches your search profile. We compare the job title against your target title and its variations. Higher = closer match to what you want.

80+ Great match 50-79 Related Under 50 Weak

Trust flags suspicious postings. We check for red flags like vague descriptions, missing company info, or spam patterns.

70+ Looks legit Under 40 Be careful

Direct Apply

Jobs with ✓ Apply Direct link straight to the company's own career page, not through a job board. You apply directly with the employer.

Skill Tags

Colored tags show skills and tools pulled from each job description so you can quickly spot what's required.

Languages Data Cloud Frameworks AI/ML

Smart Search

Type a job title in the search box and it finds related roles too. For example, searching "Data Analyst" also shows Business Analyst, Data Scientist, and similar positions.

Quick Actions

Click a job title to open it (marks as viewed). Hit ☆ Save to bookmark jobs you're interested in — view them anytime by clicking the Saved counter or choosing "Saved" in the Status filter. Use Applied? to track jobs you applied to. Hit × to hide jobs you're not interested in.

Export & Location

Use the Location filter to narrow results by city, state, or "Remote". Click Export CSV to download your current filtered results as a spreadsheet you can share or use for tracking.
